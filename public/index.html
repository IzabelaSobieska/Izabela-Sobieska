I am a Polish independent investor and entrepreneurial finance professional. With a deep foundation in Finance from the University of Warsaw and specialized training from LSE, I combine academic depth with practical market experience.

My career began at Deutsche Bank, where I managed asset allocation for High-Net-Worth clients. I later transitioned to Man Group, one of the world's largest independent alternative investment managers, honing my skills in quantitative modeling and global macro strategies.

Today, through CryptoVision Research, I apply a systematic, data-driven methodology to invest in BTC, ETH, and DeFi. I believe the future belongs to those who can bridge the gap between institutional stability and decentralized innovation.
