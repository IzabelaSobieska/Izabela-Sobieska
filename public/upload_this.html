About Me

I am a Polish independent investor and entrepreneurial finance professional, born in 1986 (39 years old). With a deep foundation in Finance from the University of Warsaw and specialized training from LSE, I operate at the intersection of traditional banking rigor and the emerging digital asset economy.

My career began at Deutsche Bank, where I managed asset allocation and portfolio analysis for High-Net-Worth (HNW) clients. I later transitioned to the global hedge fund Man Group as a Portfolio Analyst and Trader, where I honed my skills in quantitative modeling, risk management frameworks, and global macro strategies.

Today, I focus exclusively on the cryptocurrency and blockchain sector. Through my own venture, CryptoVision Research, I apply a systematic, data-driven methodology to invest in BTC, ETH, DeFi, and emerging technologies. I combine macro cycle analysis with on-chain data to build resilient, growth-oriented portfolios.
